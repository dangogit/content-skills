# Platform Verification Checklist

Platform mechanics change. Verify current product and official docs before production
handoff. The creator's account analytics override generic creator advice.

## Shared Checks

- media dimensions and codec accepted;
- current caption and title limits;
- current safe zones inspected on target device;
- no foreign-platform watermark;
- captions and hook readable without useful audio;
- one message and one primary CTA;
- public media MIME and duration verified before scheduling;
- planner refetch confirms every requested network.

## Instagram And Facebook

- Compare Reel and carousel performance on the creator's account.
- Use saves, shares, comments, reach, and watch metrics according to objective.
- Do not claim universal engagement thresholds.
- For named-resource CTAs, require active comment-to-DM route.

## TikTok

- Match length to story, not copied sweet spot.
- Verify photo-mode and video requirements in current product.
- Treat sounds and trends as creative context, not guaranteed distribution boost.

## YouTube Shorts

- Square or vertical uploads up to three minutes are categorized as Shorts under
  current official rules.
- `#Shorts` is optional.
- Third-party claimed music can block Shorts longer than one minute.
- Verify returned provider type instead of inferring from filename.

Official reference: `https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/15424877`

## Experiment Record

For every platform-specific recommendation, record source, checked date, confidence,
and expiry. If no source exists, label it `account hypothesis`.
